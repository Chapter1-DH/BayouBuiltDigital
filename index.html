About BayouBuilt

Local roots. Digital edge.

BayouBuilt Digital is a South Louisiana web studio run by DeAnna P. Hagan, Michael D. Hagan, and Michael Collins. We build websites for local businesses that deserve more than a template — businesses with a story, a community, and a reputation worth showing online.

We specialize in small businesses, service providers, and local brands across Vermilion Parish and the surrounding area. If you serve South Louisiana, we build for you.
